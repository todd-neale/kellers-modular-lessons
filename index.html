Discover the foundations of synthesis and learn to create your own sounds from the ground up in this hands-on beginners course at SchneidersKeller. The only space in London where you can explore modular hardware in person and learn directly from industry professionals.

Whether you are completely new to synthesis or a curious hobbyist looking to deepen your understanding, this course will give you the knowledge and confidence to approach any modular system and begin making music with intention, clarity and creativity.

What you will learn?

Across four guided sessions, you will build a practical understanding of:

  • What sound is and how waveforms behave.
  • Core synthesis tools including oscillators, filters, VCAs, envelopes, modulation and more.
  • How control voltage, gates and triggers work and why they matter.
  • How to create and patch a complete synth voice from scratch.
  • Ways to add movement, dynamics and expression using LFOs and modulation sources.
"This course focuses on outcomes rather than a technical syllabus. By the end, you will understand both how to build sounds and why synthesis works."

Who is this course for?

  • Complete beginners with no prior experience of synthesis.
  • Musicians who want to understand the fundamentals properly.
  • Anyone who wants hands on access to hardware in a supportive environment.
  • People curious about sound design and electronic performance.
No equipment required!

Why learn with us?

SchneidersKeller is the only dedicated space in London where you can learn modular synthesis on real hardware. You will be guided by people who work with these instruments every day.

You will use a wide range of high quality modular systems, gaining practical skills that software alone cannot provide.

Our ethos is rooted in community, experimentation and hands on discovery.
